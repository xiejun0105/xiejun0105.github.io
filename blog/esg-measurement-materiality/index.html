Measuring What Matters in ESG

Notes on measurement, financial materiality, and the limits of ESG ratings when research questions cross markets and institutions.

Draft page. Replace this outline with the finished essay when ready.

Planned angle

This note will discuss why ESG measurement is difficult, why disagreement among ratings can be informative, and how materiality depends on research design and institutional context.

Possible structure

  • What ESG ratings try to measure.
  • Why measurement disagreement is not just noise.
  • How financial materiality changes by industry and region.
  • How researchers can make ESG measurement more transparent.
