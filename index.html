Corporate sustainability · Human capital · ESG

Jun Xie

Assistant Professor, Kyushu University

I study how corporate sustainability, human capital, and ESG practices shape firm value, market efficiency, and social well-being across countries and regions.

My research uses global data to examine the links between ESG performance, individual well-being, sustainable finance, and urban prosperity, with implications for investment, management, and policy decision-making.
